Research

My current research interests include geometric & topological deep learning, graph neural networks, deep generative modelling, and their applications in computational biology (such as drug discovery and biological foundation models for DNA, RNA, and proteins). In the past, I also did research on Bayesian nonparametrics, focusing specifically on Gaussian processes and Bayesian optimization.
